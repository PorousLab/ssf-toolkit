## Quality Control Notes

1. **E. coli WR1** strain used consistently across all experiments
2. **Triplicates:** All biochemical analyses performed in triplicate
3. **Temperature:** Lab experiments at 19-23°C; pilot at ambient indoor
4. **Influent:** Tap water from Waternet (pre-treated drinking water)
5. **Flow rate:** 15 cm/h for mini/midi; variable for pilot
